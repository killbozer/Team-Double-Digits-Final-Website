Enrollment Form

Gray Primary School will accept all students whose families are residents of Gray. For students whose families reside outside of this suburb enrolment is dependant on the capacity of the school to cater for the children's educational needs.

Enrollment Procedure:

  • Enrolment Forms, Enrolment Policy and Parent Prospectus/Handbook are available at the front office.
  • Parents of potential Primary students make an appointment with the Principal. Preschool enrolments are with the Assistant Principal. Reports or assessments that relate to their childs educational/health needs or progress, and evidence of date of birth and immunisation be brought to the pre-enrolment appointment for discussion.
  • School Prospectus/Handbook is discussed, in particular an explanation of the essential partnership between school and home related to the Behaviour Management Policy.
  • Previous school is contacted to gain information which supports class placement and educational need in consultation with relevant Gray Primary School staff.
  • Student is enrolled or, where families are residents outside of Gray parents are notified of decision as soon as possible by phone.
  • Enrolment Form is entered onto Student Administration Management System.
  • New students start in class at the beginning of the next school day in uniform.
