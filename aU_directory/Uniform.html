A Compulsory Act

A school uniform is a compulsory rule for every student that are within their Primary Schooling years, as per the NT Government Department Policy. Uniforms support self-esteem, promote school team spirit and are designed for economy and convenience.

Additionally, the uniforms help represent the school when students are on an excursion, and thus, students will NOT be allowed to participate in excursions if they are not wearing the respective uniform.

Where to Purchase our Uniforms

Our uniforms are sold at the Front Office, which is the main building that handles any enquiries or enrollments.
