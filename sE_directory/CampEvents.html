Year 5/6 Camping Events

Annual School Camps

Students in Years 5 and 6 participate in an annual school camp. It is on this excursion that students engage in outdoor adventure activities focusing on bonding, strengthening relationships and team-building with peers. There is also a strong focus on building confidence, resilience and persistence before entering Middle School.

In the past this camp has been held at The Batchelor Outdoor Education Unit approximately 100km south of Darwin. Activities that students are involved in include; mountain bike riding, rock climbing, orienteering, canoeing, snorkeling, bushwalking, low and high ropes and many more. Another option for this overnight camp is the Territory Wildlife Park, activities students may be involved in at this camp include; toad busts, night train tours, bushwalking, spotlighting, behind the scenes animal feeding, animal encounters and much more.
