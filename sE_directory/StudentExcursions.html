Student Excursions

Excursions

Excursions are an important and valuable part of the curriculum.

There are three types of excursions:

  • Educational excursions related to topics of work covered in class
  • Overnight camps for Year 5/6 classes; and
  • End of year excursions which reward students for following the 5 Keys.

If there are any issues of student safety on an excursion then a parent or carer will be required to attend.
